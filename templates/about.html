About Task Organizer

Hi there! Task Organizer is my project designed to help you stay on top of your tasks and manage your time better.

Key features include:

  • Adding, editing, and deleting tasks.
  • Setting deadlines and priorities.
  • Viewing tasks in a calendar or list format.
  • Filtering tasks based on different criteria.
  • Sharing tasks with others to collaborate effectively.

Thank you for using my app! I hope it makes organizing your tasks easier and more enjoyable.
